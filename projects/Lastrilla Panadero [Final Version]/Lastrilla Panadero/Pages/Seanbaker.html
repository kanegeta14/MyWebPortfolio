King Baker

Lore

In the kingdom of Dasmonium Ruins, there was a prophecy that foretold the birth of a child who would be born from the ruins themselves. The child was destined to possess a great power that would make them the ruler of the kingdom and restore its former glory. As time passed, the prophecy was all but forgotten until one day, a child was born to a peasant family who lived among the ruins. This child was named Sean Baker, and he grew up in poverty and hardship, living among the ruins with his family. However, Sean Baker had a natural gift for music, and he spent every spare moment practicing his saxophone. One day, while practicing in a remote corner of the ruins, Sean Baker discovered a powerful new technique that would change his life forever.Sean Baker quickly rose to power, using Hellmony to subdue his enemies and bring order to the chaotic ruins. He became known as King Baker, and his rule brought prosperity and peace to the kingdom of Dasmonium Ruins.
